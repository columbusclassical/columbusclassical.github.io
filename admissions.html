Admissions Process

Application and enrollment for the 2023–2024 school year opens October 3, 2022 with an “early application” period. During early application, all paperwork is filed with Columbus Classical Academy (please refer to the application for a complete list of required documents) and, if necessary, your family interview is scheduled and conducted. No money is due, other than the non-refundable application fee of $25 per child, until your application has been accepted in March, 2023.

Early application is recommended, as it secures a spot in your child’s grade while also giving you the opportunity to decide if Columbus Classical Academy is the right fit for your family. Early application for our first year in operation also assists us. The earlier interested families apply, the easier it will be for us to finalize tuition and to evaluate whether additional sections and teachers at any given grade level may be needed.

Early application will end January 15, 2023. Beginning January 16, 2023, the non-refundable application fee increases to $50 per student.

The application period ends February 15, 2023, with all interviews finished by February 28, 2023. Admission decisions will be released during the first two weeks of March, with all preliminary acceptances posted by March 15, 2023. By this time, if it has not already been officially released, our final tuition number will be confirmed and a deposit of $500 will be due by 5:00 p.m. on Friday, March 24, 2023 to secure your spot. After March 24th, applications will be accepted and enrollment will continue on a rolling cycle. If your deposit is not received by 5:00 p.m. on March 24, 2023, your slot will be opened up to the next person on the waiting list.

Waiting List

If all spots in your child’s grade are filled and you have otherwise satisfied the admissions criteria, your child will be placed on a waiting list. Admissions will continue on a rolling cycle after March 24, 2023. During rolling admission, as spots become available, the next person on the waiting list will be admitted. No deposit or tuition is due until you have been notified of your acceptance from the waiting list. A deposit of $500 is due within 48 hours of acceptance. If the deposit is not received, the spot will be released to the next person on the waiting list.

March 1–15, 2023 Admission decisions released

March 24, 2023 Enrollment deposit due

Application

This section provides all information needed to submit an application to Columbus Classical Academy. Please contact our admissions team at admissions@columbusclassical.org with any questions.

Step 1: Complete Paperwork

Complete Paperwork: please print and fill out neatly or use the fillable PDF.

  • Parent & Family Information (one per family)
  • Application (one per student)
    • Grade K–5 Application
    • Grade 6–8 Application
  • Report card (one per student from prior year; if homeschooled, include curriculum used)
  • Standardized test results (one per student from prior year)
  • Include application fee:
    • Early Application (October 3, 2022 to January 15, 2023): $25 per student
    • General Application (January 15, 2023 to February 15, 2023): $50 per student
    • Make checks payable to Columbus Classical Academy
  • Mail to: Columbus Classical Academy, P.O. Box #2, Worthington, OH 43085
  • If you prefer to submit your application electronically, please email all required documents to admissions@columbusclassical.org and pay your application fee here.

Step 2: Email Confirmation

You will receive the following email confirmations from Columbus Classical Academy.

  • Confirmation that an application paperwork has been received
  • Confirmation that application is complete and under review by the admissions team

Step 3: Information Session, Interview, Screening

  • Attend a CCA Information Night (see the Events Page)
  • We will reach out to schedule a family interview
  • We will reach out to schedule kindergarten screening (if required)

Step 4: Admissions Decision

Admissions decisions will be provided on the following dates:

  • Early Application: mid-February 2023
  • General Application: mid-March 2023

Step 5: Enrollment Decision

  • Submit non-refundable enrollment deposit of $500 per student by 5:00 pm.m March 24, 2023
  • Schedule Math placement test for any student 5th grade and higher
  • Submit a recent photo of each child enrolling

Step 6: Select Tuition Payment Plan

  • Payment in full: 100% paid by August 1, 2023, receives a 3% discount
  • 2 Payments: 60% due August 1, 2023, 40% due December 31, 2023
  • 9 Payments: monthly payment August 1, 2023 through May 1, 2024, service fee will apply

Step 5: Final Paperwork

Turn in final paperwork by August 1, 2023, including:

  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Immunization records or Letter of Exemption
  • Health questionnaire and Medication Permission Form (if applicable)
  • Emergency Contact form (one per family)
  • Student Parking Permit form (when applicable)
  • Signature page of CCA’s Honor Code (one per student)
  • Family Handbook acknowledgement (one per family)

Tuition & Fees

Because we believe that a Columbus Classical Academy education is good for every student, we endeavor to make it affordable to as many families as possible. At the same time, we also have decided not to accept direct government funding that is typically available even to other private schools, in order to maintain the integrity of our Mission and the independence of the institution we are founding. As a result, 2023–2024 tuition will depend, in part, upon enrollment numbers and donor generosity.

We will post final tuition numbers no later than March 15, 2023, before any enrollment deposits are due.
