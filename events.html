Schedule a Visit

We are grateful for your interest in Columbus Classical Academy and encourage you to plan a visit to our campus, either during the school day or for a weekend information session and tour. There’s no better way to get to know a school than to see it for yourself. We look forward to meeting you!

School Day Visits

Details

On Tuesday mornings, we welcome visitors who would like to observe CCA in action. Registration is required, and visitors must arrive and sign in at the main office not later than 7:50am to attend the morning assembly.

Registered visitors join the whole CCA community for a brief (5-10 min) morning assembly to start the day, which includes a prayer, the Pledge of Allegiance, a patriotic song, and recitation of the Honor Code.

Following morning assembly, visitors meet with Mr. Gibson for a small-group information session. The visit concludes with a tour of campus during which guests have an opportunity to observe briefly a few classes in session.

If you would like to register for a school-day visit, please email ccampbell@columbusclassical.org or click the link below and identify the date you would like to visit. Please note: Attendance at school-day visits is limited to 8 guests, and any late arrivals will be required join the group only after the conclusion of the morning assembly.

Information Sessions & Tours

Details

Everyone is welcome to attend one of our information sessions. Information sessions are held the first and third Thursdays of the month in the form of Lunch and Learns or Classics and Conversation events. RSVP is required to attend, and space is limited. Please RSVP to the dates listed below. Please contact Janine King with any questions, jking@columbusclassical.org.

We ask that you arrive at least 5 minutes prior to the scheduled start time. Visitors will have an opportunity to learn about the mission, curriculum, and educational philosophy of Columbus Classical Academy, meet and speak with Mr. Gibson our Head of School, ask questions, and tour the campus (tours are during Classics and Conversation events only).

We also offer drop in tour times on Tuesday mornings from 9:00 to 10:00 am and Thursday afternoons from 1:00 to 2:00 pm. No RSVP is required—just drop in!
